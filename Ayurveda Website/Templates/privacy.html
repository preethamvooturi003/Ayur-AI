Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Research Overview

This data collection is part of an academic research study conducted by student researchers from VIT university. While data collection was facilitated at Meenakshi Hospital Jagtial, this is an independent research project and not affiliated with or endorsed by the Meenakshi Hospital Jagtial.

Data Collection and Usage

  • This research project collects health-related data solely for academic research purposes.
  • All data is collected with explicit written consent from participants.
  • Participation is entirely voluntary.
  • No personally identifiable information is stored with the health data.
  • Data collected will be used only for research analysis and academic publications.

Data Protection

  • All collected data is stored securely using encryption.
  • Access to the data is strictly limited to authorized research team members.
  • Data will be anonymized before any analysis or publication.
  • Raw data will be destroyed after the completion of the research project.

Participant Rights

  • Participants have the right to withdraw their data from the study at any time.
  • Participants can request to see what data is held about them.
  • Participants can request deletion of their data at any time.
  • Questions about data handling can be directed to preetham.vooturi@outlook.com

Research Ethics

This research has been approved by Prof.Arivoli A(arivoli.a@vit.ac.in) VIT University. follows all applicable research ethics guidelines and data protection regulations.
